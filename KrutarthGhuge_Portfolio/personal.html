My Bio

Hi there!
I am Krutarth Ghuge, aged 21. I was born in the suburban region of the metropolitan city of Mumbai, India. I completed my kindergarten years of schooling in Mumbai itself, after which me and my mother moved to Nashik, a developing city then in the same state, Maharashtra. I then completed the rest of my schooling upto High School from the school Fravashi International Academy. Wherein following the British pattern education system from the British Council, I completed the International General Certificate of Secondary Education (IGCSE) and Advanced Level (A LEVEL) examinations. Upon the successful completion of my High School education, I have moved to Canada to pursue my Bachelor's degree. I am currently in my 3rd year of education at Trent University, Peterborough, Canada; completing my Bachelor of Science (Honours) degree in Computer Science, specializing in Data Analytics, as well as doing a minor in Economics.

I come from a close-knit family, with just a few members, my mother, my grandfather and grandmother. My mom's sister also used to stay with us, though after marriage she moved out. Family and relations matter to me a lot as I have not had a lot of people around me throughout - my dad passed away when I was about 4 months old, and since then it has been my mother taking the workload of two parents. Moving forward I aim to achieve my desired goals with perfection in order to meet my family's and my own expectations.
